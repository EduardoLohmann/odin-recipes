Chocolate Cream

Description

A simple and delicious chocolate cream that goes as a filling just as well as a topping

Ingredients

  • 1 Chocolate bar
  • 200g Heavy cream

Preparartions

  1. Melt the chocolate in the microwave on a 15 seconds interval
  2. Mix between ssets of heating until completely melted
  3. Add the heavy cream and mix until combined

That is all! It`s a very simple and useful recipe to know as an add-on for other, more complex, desserts. You can use milk or dark chocolate as you taste for sweetnes calls.
