Cookies

Description

Who doesn`n loe a good ol` cookie? There is no fancy ingredients or big innovation here, just plain, old, good blessed cookies!

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Refined Sugar
  • Brown Sugar
  • Baking powder
  • Egg
  • Butter
  • Chocolate chips

Preparartions

  1. Mix the flour, both suggars and baking powder in a bowl
  2. Melt the butter and add it to the mixture
  3. Beat the egg separatly and the add it tho the mixture
  4. Add the chocolate chips
  5. In a tray, form little balls of dough and space the well, use a paper sheet
  6. Put it in the oven for approximately 15 minutes at 200 degress celcius
  7. Leave it to cool for about 10 minutes
  8. Eat it while still warm

Nothing like a classic. The dough can be stored in the freezer to be used on another day, just a quick heating up for a heavenly experience.
