Chicken Sandwich

Description

A healthy chicken sandwich recepi with a lot of vegetables and salads!

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Chicken Tights
  • Purple Onion
  • Lettuce
  • Carrots
  • Greek Yogurt
  • Garlic
  • Eggs
  • Mustard

Preparartions

Light Mayo

The mayo is optional since it can be quite time consuming

  1. Boil the eggs for about 12 minutes
  2. Slice and mince the garlic into very fine pieces
  3. In a bowl, put on the yogurt, egg yolk, minced garlic and mustard
  4. Mix thoroughly until everything is combined and the yolks have dissolved
  5. Store it in the fridge until ready to use

Main sandwich

  1. Bake the chicken tichts in the oven for proximately 40 minutes at 200 degrees celcius
  2. Slice the lettuce
  3. Slice the carrots
  4. Slice the onions
  5. Remove the chicken when it`s finished
  6. Cut it into blocks
  7. Cut the cread in half
  8. Put on about 2 tablespoons of mayo and spread it evenly (optional)
  9. Put the lettuce
  10. Then the chicken
  11. Then the carrots
  12. Then the onions
  13. And finally, the egg whites, if you boiled it!
  14. Close the sandwich (a little violence may be required)

That`s it! Your sandwich is done and ready to be eaten. The calories in it depend mainly on the bread, mayo and chicken, a standart portion will be about 500kcal per sandwich. But remember, you can fill it with a lot of veggies to volume up quite a lot to leave you well satiated.
